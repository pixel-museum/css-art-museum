Frequently Asked Questions

General

What is CSS Art Museum?

CSS Art Museum is a digital platform showcasing art created entirely with CSS.

Is it free to use?

Yes! All browsing, profile creation, and submissions are free.

For Artists & Creators

How do I submit my artwork?

Sign up, click "Submit Artwork," and paste your HTML/CSS code with a title and description.

Account & Technical

I forgot my password. What do I do?

Use the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page to reset it via email.
